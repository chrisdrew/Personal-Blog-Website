Why re-learn?

After three years (I think) away from coding anything I have decided to come back into my old field! I feel like now is a good time to get back into it. Now lets re-wind a little and see what I have done in the past.

So when I first started I was a developer for an e-learning company and we used Flash (AS2), and Director! Wow I am old :/. Working with this company I have worked for some top clients like:

  • SmartBaords
  • RCMP
  • Thunderbird

After ten hard years with the company I got the bad news that I was being laid off along with six other people. I took it hard, but I walked out the door with a smile on my face saying goodbye to everyone I worked with. My wife was super amazing and helped me get back on my feet.

It took some months but I decided to go back into school this time designing/building in a different way. I decided to become a carpenter. I always loved building with my hands, so I thought why not give it a go. I passed the six month course feeling good about myself and seeing myself working for a big company. I am now working at PCL, building a new hospital in Calgary for people with cancer. I am very proud of the work I am doing there, it makes me happy.

But now it is time for a change again!

I never loose. I either win or learn.

- Nelson Mandala
